ORMIR community

Open and Reproducible Musculoskeletal Imaging Research community



Our goals


  • Create open, reproducible, well-tested, and well-documented code to analyze musculoskeletal (MSK) images
  • Standardize data acquisition and management to favor data sharing and algorithm comparison
  • Promote a culture of openness and reproducibility in MSK imaging research for a faster advancement of the field

Our values


  • Be inclusive, friendly, and patient
  • Be welcoming, considerate, and respectful
  • Be careful in the words you choose. Be kind
  • Moderate your expectations. We are all busy. Different people contribute differently
  • When we disagree, try to understand why. A simple apology can go a long way.
We adapted these principles from the Project Jupyter Code of Conduct
