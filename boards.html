Advisors & Outreach

We have two advisory boards: the Community Advisory Board and the Technical Advisory Board, and one outreach team: the Social Media Team!

Community Advisory Board

Our role is to coordinate community directions and outreach activities. We create guidelines for our community, provide recommendations, and plan gatherings. We are the philosophers!

Technical Advisory Board

Our role is provide directions and support for code standardization and homogenization. Our outcome include templates for Jupyter Notebooks and GitHub readme files, coding guidelines, and the package ORMIR-MIDS for data structure. We are the ravers!
